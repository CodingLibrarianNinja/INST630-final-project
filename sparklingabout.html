About Stephani

Stephani grew up in the Richmond, Virginia area. She began developing her creativity through taking several arts/craft classes in high school. When she attended James Madison University, Stephani earned both a bachelors and master's degree in history. Since 2006, Stephani began making jewelry on the side as a welcome divertion from her studies. Currently, she continues to live in the Harrisonburg, Virginia area, working at Massanutten Resort in the reservations department. Also, Stephani is an online library science graduate student at the University of Maryland, in hopes of becoming a reference librarian. Besides jewelry designing, Stephani's other interests include reading, hiking, photography, volunteering at her church and local library,strategy/puzzle board games, and spending time with family/friends.

About Sparkling Creations Jewelry

Stephani received her first official jewelry making lessons from her friend Melanie, a skilled jewelry designer, in 2009. In addition to Melanie's lessons, Stephani also consulted online tutorials and books from her local library for beginner jewelry making patterns/tutorials. As her jewelry making skills increased, friends and family started asking Stephani to design jewelry pieces for them. Inspired by their encouragement, Stephani started an online Etsy shop and applied for her small business licence in December 2010. She named her business "Sparkling Creations Jewelry" due to her love of bright colors and shiny beading materials!

Since 2010, Stephani has participated in almost fifteen craft fairs/festivals including the Dayton Autumn Celebration and various shows at the Rockingham County Fairgrounds. In addition, she sells her jewelry pieces on consignment at both the Hopkins Cabin in Massanutten and the Silver Lake Mill in Dayton. For up to date information about Stephani's business, go to the Sparkling Creations Jewelry Facebook Page

Jewelry Making Techniques

For the majority of her jewelry pieces, Stephani uses intermediate to advanced wire wrapped techniques to create them. Through manipulating the wire, she can create a variety of jewelry pieces including rings, bracelets, necklaces, earrings, and pendants. In addition to wire wrapping the links, she completely handcrafts all of the clasps, ear wires, pendant frames, and other findings for her pieces. Only high quality materials are used for each piece including tarnish resistant argentium silver/copper wire so her pieces will stay shiny longer than regular silver/copper. Other materials include Swarovski Crystals, natural gemstones, and cane glass beads. Custom bridal and birthstone jewelry pieces are available upon request as well. Each unique handcrafted jewelry piece is perfect for any occasion!
